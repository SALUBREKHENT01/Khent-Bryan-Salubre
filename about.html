BSKE2023 in BARANGAY MAGSAYSAY,PLACER SDN

Barangay and Sangguniang Kabataan Election 2023 in Barangay Magsaysay is a very intense fight during Campaign period until the Election day.Election was being held at Placer West Central Elementary School and it's start at 7o'clock in the morning until 3o'clock in the afternoon.There are 37 candidates who run in barangay Magsaysay. 2 aspirants of Punong Barangay, 16 aspirants in Barangay Kagawad, 3 aspirants in SK Chairperson and 17 aspirants for SK Kagawad. All candidates was very nervous for what is the outcome in said activity especially in the counting hours.
